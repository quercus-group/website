Wadim Baslow

Wadim is a project manager at Quercus Group. He designs and implements projects from A to Z with a strategic, structured and diplomatic approach and mindset.

At heart, Wadim loves working on the ground, where he meets real people in their context. It makes him a great observer and curious facilitator. Wadim’s greatest asset is his ability to create a space for collaboration and sharing, where ideas and solutions sprout. He worked together with with foreign ministries and big business organisations. His thematic strongholds are sustainability, cities and systems change.
