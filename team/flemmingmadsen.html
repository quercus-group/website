Flemming Madsen

Flemming is an associate at Quercus Group. He has worked with public-private partnerships and innovation clusters for the past 35 years, organising the Danish Sound Technology and Danish Lighting networks. In 2010 he co-developed the renowned DOLL Green Labs in Albertslund, Denmark, and he has been working for GATE21, the leading Green Transition and Sustainable Growth Agency of the Greater Copenhagen Union. In this capacity, he complements Quercus' work on living labs.
